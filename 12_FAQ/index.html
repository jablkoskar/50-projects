What do frogs eat?

Frogs eat insects, small animals, and sometimes even other frogs!

How do frogs breathe?

Frogs can breathe through their skin, lungs, and the lining of their mouth.

Why do frogs croak?

Frogs croak to attract mates and to mark their territory.

Can frogs live in both water and on land?

Yes, frogs are amphibians and can live in both aquatic and terrestrial environments.

How do frogs protect themselves from predators?

Frogs use camouflage, toxic skin, and quick jumps to escape predators.
